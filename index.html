What really matters, at a glance.

The Smart Plant M1 delivers real-time measurements of soil moisture, ambient light, temperature, and humidity—critical parameters that directly impact plant health.

Minimalist design, maximum clarity.

The Smart Plant’s e-paper display combines timeless elegance with unparalleled readability. View essential plant data at a glance, even in bright sunlight, without sacrificing the beauty of your indoor space.

Care for your plants while caring for the planet.

Crafted from recycled plastics and powered by a long-lasting rechargeable battery, the M1 is designed with sustainability in mind. Our commitment to environmental responsibility extends throughout the product lifecycle—from materials to packaging and beyond.

Any plant, any pot.
